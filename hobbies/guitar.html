Guitar Chords

Guitar chords are a fundamental part of music. Guitar chords are basically played with 2 or more simultaneously notes that creates a certain type of progression. Chords can be really simple, or they can be so hard that it bleeds out your fingers (Seriously... Personal experience!) There are many variations of chords, like open chords, barre chords, and power chords. Some of the more easier chords is the Am chord, or the Em chord. These chords only require 2 to 3 fingers in order to play this chord.
